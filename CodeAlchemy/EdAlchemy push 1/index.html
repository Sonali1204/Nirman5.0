What Is EdAlchemy?

EdAlchemy is an AI-driven academic, productivity, and placement ecosystem designed to support students throughout their entire learning journey. It organizes study material, generates quizzes, improves resumes, detects repeated PYQs, and provides personalized placement roadmaps.
